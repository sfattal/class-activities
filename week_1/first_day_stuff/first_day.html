BONUS

PB & J

  1. You will need: toast, peanut butter, jam, a knife, a toaster
  2. Remove 2 slices of precut toast from the bag of toast
  3. Place each slice in a separate slot of the toaster, and toast to your liking
  4. Using the knife, spread the desired amount of peanut butter on one slice of toast
  5. Wipe down the knife with a paper towel
  6. Using the knife, spread the desired amount of jam on the other slice of toast
  7. Combine both slices with the toppings on the inside
